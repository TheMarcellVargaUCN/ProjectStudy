American Versus European Education Fee

According to College Board, published tuition fees for 2018/19 at state colleges are an average of US$10,230 for state residents, and $26,290 for everyone else. This compares to an average of $35,830 at private non-profit colleges, but it’s also possible to study in the US at a much lower outlay.

Tuition fees at public universities in France have variable prices, depending on your chosen study level. For Bachelor's programmes, the average tuition fees are around 170 EUR per year. So when we add all prices for accommodation and living by itself we can get to 10 000$ per one year.
