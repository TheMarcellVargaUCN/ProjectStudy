So your application has been approved, you’ve got your offer and you’re ready to go overseas. But wait!
Before you can leave the country, you’re going to need to make sure you have an International Student Visa.

Don’t worry, this is something ESS can arrange on your behalf from start to finish. Not only do we provide
up-to-date advice regarding the visa requirements of your destination country but we can process the visas in-house.

This means you don’t need to go outside ESS to complete this part of the process. And because we know the details
of your application already, we can help get you quicker outcomes.
